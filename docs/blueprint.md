# **App Name**: Bhandari Landing

## Core Features:

- Product Interest Form (Step 1): Capture the user's primary furniture interest (Outdoor, Office, etc.) and project scope (single piece, full collection).
- Contact Information Form (Step 2): Collect user's name, email, phone number, and any additional comments they provide for personalized assistance.
- Lead Data Storage: Securely store user-submitted form data (product interest and contact details) for effective lead management.
- Personalized Response Tool: Leverage generative AI to provide customized collection information and design team introductions based on form responses.
- Visual Showcase: Display high-impact furniture visuals with unique and discrete styling.
- Core Value Highlight: Emphasize design, function, and quality as the foundation of product development.
- Trust Element Presentation: Showcase competence, service, sustainability, and dependability to establish brand trust.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Earthy brown (#A67B5B) to represent heritage, craftsmanship, and natural materials.
- Background color: Creamy beige (#F5F5DC), a light neutral providing contrast and a calm atmosphere.
- Accent color: Warm gold (#D4A27A) for highlights, buttons and calls to action, connoting elegance and sophistication.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' serif, with an elegant, fashionable, high-end feel. Body font: 'PT Sans' for the body to enhance readability.
- Hero section with full-width impactful furniture imagery.
- Two-step form embedded within the primary call to action section.
- Dedicated sections for trust elements, contact information, and social media links.
